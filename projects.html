GS-170 Career Development

In this course, students develop resources, professional connections and essential employability skills to obtain or improve employment in a field related to their university certificate. Students will network and apply for job opportunities in their industry. The life-long skills gained in this course are valuable for both active and future job seekers.

Course Learning Outcomes

1.Communicate personal and essential employable skills matched to a target job.

2.Create a list of employers, contacts and opportunities to obtain or improve employment.

3.Prepare effective professional documents and digital profile that incorporate essential employability skills.

4.Identify and practice effective networking and job-search strategies.

5.Connect and network with professionals, mentors and potential employers in student's target industry.

6.Apply for jobs and/or improved position in current organization.

7.Demonstrate effective interviewing and post-interviewing skills.
